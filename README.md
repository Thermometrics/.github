## About Thermometrics Corporation

[Thermometrics Corporation](https://thermometricscorp.com) has manufactured temperature sensors and custom thermal measurement hardware since 1965. The company supports custom thermocouples, RTDs, bearing sensors, thermowells, fittings, accessories, calibration support, and documentation-driven programs from its U.S.-based manufacturing operation in Northridge, California.

Customers often work with Thermometrics when a standard catalog probe is not enough. Common requirements include custom sheath materials, special fittings, lead exits, bearing sensor tip geometry, calibration points, material traceability, dimensional inspection, and documentation aligned to customer quality systems.
